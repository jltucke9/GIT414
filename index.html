About Me

My name is Jeanne Tucker. I am a design-focused web development student experienced in UX research, responsive front-end development, motion design, and Adobe Creative Suite. Interested in work that values creativity, empathy, and impact, including branding, product design, and social-purpose organizations, with a background in special education and a passion for creating human-centered designs and visually compelling work.

I have experience working within Adobe Suite and Figma. I've worked on design projects that included things like social media graphics, print materials, motion pieces, and brand design. I know how to work within a style guide, build a design from ideation to development, and take feedback without taking it personally.
